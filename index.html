Glazing technology

Five layers between
you and the weather.

Anatomy of a Vitralis triple-glazed unit. Every layer has a job — and we use the best materials for each.

01
Outer pane · 4 mm float
Tempered low-iron glass with hydrophobic coating — rain runs off cleanly, dirt doesn't stick.
02
Argon-filled chamber · 16 mm
Argon is 6× denser than air — slowing thermal conduction. Sealed with double-barrier butyl + polysulfide for 25-year tightness.
03
Center pane · low-E coated
Magnetron-sputtered silver coating reflects infrared back into the room while letting visible light through.
04
Argon chamber #2 · 16 mm
Second insulating chamber. Warm-edge spacer (Swisspacer Ultimate) — 70% less heat bridging than aluminium.
05
Inner pane · low-E + safety
Optional laminated safety film holds glass together if broken — required for floor-to-ceiling installations.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

How accurate is the configurator price?

Within ±5% of the final invoice. The price you see includes the profile, glazing, sashes, color and standard fittings. Installation, removal of old windows, and unusual openings (arches, trapezoids) are quoted separately after a free site visit.

What's the difference between 60 / 70 / 82 mm profiles?

Profile depth determines the number of insulating chambers and the maximum glazing thickness. 60 mm fits standard double glazing (Uw ~1.3); 70 mm is our balanced choice for triple glazing (Uw ~0.9); 82 mm allows the thickest triple units with Uw down to 0.7 — the passive-house standard.

How long does delivery take?

14 working days on average for standard configurations across the EU. Custom colors (RAL or wood-laminate) add 5–7 days. Installation is scheduled separately and typically takes 1–2 hours per window.

Do you measure on site?

Yes — for any order over €2,000 we send a certified surveyor for free. They take laser measurements, photograph the opening and confirm the technical solution. The configurator price is then locked.

What's covered by the 30-year warranty?

Profile material (no yellowing, cracking or warping), color stability, gasket seal integrity, and hardware function. Glass units carry a separate 25-year seal warranty against fogging.

Can I finance an order?

Yes — interest-free financing up to 24 months on orders over €1,500, subject to credit check. Orders over €5,000 qualify for 36-month plans at 0.9% APR.
